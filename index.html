🔒 SplitSmart Privacy Policy

Version 1.1 Effective Date: April 17, 2026 · Last Updated: June 12, 2026

SplitSmart ("the App", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in with Google or create an Email/Password account, we receive:

  • Display name and email address — used to identify you within groups.
  • Profile photo URL (Google Sign-In only) — displayed in the app UI.

We do not store your password. Authentication is handled entirely by Firebase Authentication.

If you join a group as a guest (without creating an account), an anonymous Firebase session is created. It contains no email or personal details — only the display name you choose for the group. Orphaned guest sessions are periodically deleted.

1.2 Financial Data

You enter personal transactions, group expenses, budget limits, saving goals, and subscription details. This data is:

  • Stored locally on your device in an encrypted database (SQLCipher, AES-256). The encryption key is generated on your device and kept in the platform secure store (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain) — it never leaves your device.
  • Synced to Google Cloud Firestore when you are signed in, enabling cross-device access.

We never sell, share, or monetize your financial data.

1.3 Receipt Images

If you attach receipt photos to expenses, they are:

  • Processed on-device by Google ML Kit for text recognition (OCR). No image data is sent to any server for OCR.
  • Optionally uploaded to Firebase Cloud Storage so they are accessible across your devices.

1.4 Analytics (Privacy-Safe)

We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand app usage and fix crashes. We track feature usage only:

  • Examples: "group_created", "expense_added", "theme_toggled", "language_changed".
  • No financial amounts, no names, no descriptions, no personally identifiable information (PII) is ever logged to analytics.

1.5 Push Notification Tokens

If you grant notification permission, we store your Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device token in Firestore to send you push notifications when a group member adds an expense. Tokens are automatically cleaned up when they become invalid.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Core functionality: Splitting bills, tracking personal finances, syncing data across devices.
  • Push notifications: Alerting you when a group member adds an expense or records a settlement.
  • Crash reporting: Diagnosing and fixing bugs to improve app stability.
  • Analytics: Understanding which features are used so we can improve the app.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Cloud data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore and Firebase Storage, protected by Firebase Security Rules that restrict access to authorized users only.
  • Local data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database (SQLCipher, AES-256) on your device. The key lives in the Android Keystore / iOS Keychain.
  • Backups you share off-device are protected by a passphrase you choose; we cannot read or recover them without it. Automatic on-device backups are encrypted with your device key.
  • All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Biometric authentication (fingerprint/FaceID) can be enabled to protect app access.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Group members: When you join a group, your name and expenses within that group are visible to other group members.
  • Firebase services: Data is processed by Google Firebase infrastructure (Auth, Firestore, Storage, Analytics, Crashlytics, Cloud Messaging) under Google's Data Processing Terms.

5. Your Rights

  • Access & Export: You can export your data as a PDF report or an encrypted backup file at any time from the Settings screen.
  • Deletion: You can delete all your data from the Settings screen ("Reset All Data"). This removes all local and cloud data.
  • Account Deletion: You can sign out at any time. To delete your Firebase account, contact us at the email below.
  • Offline Use: You can use the app without signing in. All data stays on your device.

6. Children's Privacy

SplitSmart is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
