🗑️ Delete Your Account

Account Deletion SplitSmart · Last Updated: June 20, 2026

You can permanently delete your SplitSmart account and all associated data at any time. Choose one of the methods below.

Method 1 — Delete from within the app

This is the fastest way to delete your account immediately.

1
Open the SplitSmart app on your device.
2
Tap the Settings icon (bottom navigation or profile screen).
3
Scroll to the Account section at the top.
4
Tap Delete Account and confirm when prompted.
⚠️ This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your cloud data will be deleted immediately.

Method 2 — Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app or prefer to request deletion manually, email us at:

What data gets deleted?

  • Your Firebase Authentication account (email / Google Sign-In)
  • All personal transactions, wallets, saving goals, budgets, and subscriptions stored in the cloud
  • All group memberships and shared expenses associated with your account
  • Your push notification tokens and profile information

Note: Local data stored on your device (offline mode) is not automatically removed when your account is deleted. You can clear it manually via Settings → Reset All Data before or after deletion.
